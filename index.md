# ParkLao — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026  
**Version:** 1.1  
**App:** ParkLao — Parking Management System  
**Platforms:** Android  

---

## Introduction

ParkLao ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using ParkLao, you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Phone Number** — used for account login and authentication  
- **Password** — securely stored as a hashed value  
- **Shop Name / Address / Logo (optional)** — for business identification and ticket display  

### 1.2 Usage Data

We collect limited, **anonymized usage data** to improve the app:

- App interactions (e.g., check-in, reports viewed)
- Feature usage events (anonymized)
- Crash logs for debugging and stability improvements  

👉 This data **does not identify individual users**

---

### 1.3 Parking Data (Business Data)

- Vehicle license plate numbers  
- Parking timestamps (check-in / check-out)  
- Parking fees and transaction records  

👉 This data belongs to the business user and is only used to provide the service.

---

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Provide core app functionality  
- Manage user accounts  
- Generate parking tickets and reports  
- Improve app performance and stability  
- Provide customer support  

We **do not sell or use your data for advertising**.

---

## 3. Data Storage

### Local Storage
- Data is stored locally on your device (SQLite)
- Works offline

### Cloud Storage (Pro Plan)
We use **Supabase** as a secure backend provider:

- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data is encrypted at rest
- Access is protected by secure authentication (JWT)

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not sell or share personal data**.

We only share data in limited cases:

- With **Supabase** (infrastructure provider) to operate the service  
- If required by law  
- With your consent  

---

## 5. Data Security

We protect your data using:

- Password hashing (bcrypt)  
- Secure HTTPS communication  
- Authentication tokens (JWT)  
- Access control (Row-Level Security)  

---

## 6. Your Rights

You can:

- Access your data  
- Update your data  
- Request deletion  

📧 Contact: **info@devfiw.com**

---

## 7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account by:

1. Opening the ParkLao app  
2. Going to Settings  
3. Selecting **Delete Account**  

Or contact us via email.

🗑 After deletion:
- Your data will be permanently deleted  
- Some data may be retained for up to **30 days** for backup and legal compliance  

---

## 8. Data Retention

- Free Plan: stored locally  
- Pro Plan: stored in cloud  
- Deleted accounts: removed within **30 days**
